About Me

Hello! My name is Tim LaRou and I'm currently learning web development with the University of Texas's Bootcamp program.

Prior to web development, I was a senior associate at PricewaterhouseCoopers in the international tax qualitative solutions group. I primarily did data analysis using excel and SQL.

Other than that I was in the Marine Corps for 5 years. I was first stationed in Korea for a year as a linguist. I was also deployed to Afghanistan for a year working in the intelligence field. I was discharged in 2011 as a sergeant. I then attended the University of Minnesota graduating in 2014 with a degree in Accounting.
